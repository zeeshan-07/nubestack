Modern organizations demand cloud solutions that balance flexibility, control, and cost. Nubestack delivers tailored private, public, and hybrid cloud strategies—never a one-size-fits-all approach. We use leading platforms (OpenStack, OpenShift, AWS, Azure, GCP) to design, build, and manage cloud environments optimized for your business goals.

What Are Private/Public Cloud Solutions?

Private Cloud: Dedicated, secure, and customizable—hosted on-premises or by a trusted provider. Platforms like OpenStack (IaaS) and OpenShift (PaaS) deliver full control and compliance.

Public Cloud: Scalable, pay-as-you-go services from AWS, Azure, GCP—ideal for rapid innovation and reducing infrastructure overhead.

Hybrid & Multi-Cloud: Combine private and public clouds for maximum agility. Run workloads where they fit best, with seamless management and connectivity across all platforms.

Real-World Example: Financial Services Hybrid Cloud

Example: Financial Services Hybrid Cloud A bank must keep sensitive data on-premises for compliance, but wants public cloud agility for development and customer apps. Nubestack delivers:

  • OpenStack private cloud for secure, compliant data and transactions
  • AWS for scalable, customer-facing web/mobile platforms
  • OpenShift for unified app deployment across both clouds
  • Secure networking, identity, and cost optimization spanning all environments This hybrid model ensures compliance and security, while enabling rapid innovation and scalability.
