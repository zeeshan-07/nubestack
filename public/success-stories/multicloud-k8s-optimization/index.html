Multi-Cloud Agility: Kubernetes Migration and Vendor Lock-in Reduction

How a technology provider achieved true multi-cloud portability and massive cost savings by standardizing on OpenStack and Kubernetes with NubeStack's guidance.

March 25, 2025

A growing technology provider was using multiple public clouds and experiencing escalating costs and compatibility issues between environments. NubeStack helped them define and execute a cohesive multi-cloud strategy by standardizing their application delivery on OpenStack and Kubernetes. This reduced reliance on proprietary cloud services, enabling them to move workloads seamlessly and optimize spending.

Key Results:

  • 30% reduction in annual cloud spend through better resource allocation
  • Achieved seamless application portability across three major cloud environments
  • Increased developer velocity with standardized, automated deployment pipelines
