A major financial services organization was struggling to meet stringent data sovereignty and regulatory compliance (GDPR, national laws) requirements while adopting hybrid cloud. NubeStack provided the solution by deploying **on-prem Ceph storage clusters**, ensuring sensitive data never left the client's premises while remaining accessible to public and private cloud workloads.

**Key Results:**
- 100% compliance with data residency laws
- Maintained a secure, low-latency data layer on-premises
- Enabled hybrid cloud adoption without increasing data security risk
